**33** Ten [shekels] of gold, full of incense;
one bullock of the herd,
one ram,
one lamb of a year old for a burnt offering;
**34** And one kid of the goats for a sin offering;
**35** And for a peace offering,
two heifers,
five rams,
five he-goats,
five yearling ewes.
